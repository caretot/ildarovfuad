gPAC – A Student-Centered Academic Analytics Platform

gPAC is a web-based academic platform I developed to assist students in monitoring and improving their academic performance with clarity and ease. It allows users to calculate their GPA, track progress over semesters, visualize academic rankings, and receive AI-driven feedback based on performance trends. The platform is tailored specifically for students at UFAZ, ensuring a smooth and personalized user experience. With a strong focus on data privacy, automation, and usability, gPAC empowers students to take control of their educational journey through intuitive and dynamic tools.
